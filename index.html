Purpose

Explore South Carolina’s golf courses and Signature Holes with searchable stats and community ratings. Caddy Compass gives golfers an easy way to compare courses with a focus on individual hole ratings, difficulty, slope, and yardage — all in one place.

About

Many golf websites focus on prices and tee times. Caddy Compass solves this by providing a centralized resource where golfers can compare courses directly and evaluate hole-by-hole. Signature holes are highlighted, but user participation helps complete missing data.
